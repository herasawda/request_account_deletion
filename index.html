8xFootball – Account Deletion

Your privacy is important to us

How to Delete Your Account

Users can delete their 8xFootball account at any time directly from inside the app. Once deleted, all related personal data is permanently removed from our servers.

Follow these steps:

  1. Open the 8xFootball app
  2. Go to the Profile page
  3. Tap on Request Account Deletion
  4. Your account will be deleted immediately

Data That Will Be Deleted

When users delete their account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Profile information (name, email, image)
  • Favorites
  • Voted matches
  • Earned coins
  • Unlocked tips
  • All other personal data associated with the account
